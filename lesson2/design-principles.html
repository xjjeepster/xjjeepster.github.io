Design Principles on the Web

Ryan J Hill

Visual Hierarchy

KSL News

www.ksl.com

KSL along with most news sites do a great job of displaying hierarchy. They have their main news story in a large format at the top of the screen. They have the heading and font larger then the other story's that follow. KSL will have a couple of lesser stories in a medium format below their main story. Following their medium format they will then have a list of other news articles in a small format.

PARC Contrast

RAZER Inc.

www.razer.com

Razer's website has a lot of great contrast. Their header is a subdued grey with thier menu icon in white. Thier logo is a bright green that stands out and calls notice to their branding. This bright green is seen throughout the rest of the page used as an accent in images, titles, and icons. They have a dark background with subdued text. When they want to call attention to a specific title or image, they will use lighter text and images that pop out of the dark coloring.

White Space

GOVX

www.govx.com

GOVX is a vendor for military and law enforcement equipment. They utilize white space to give space between their products and elements. This causes a very clean look even when in a mobile application. It is easy to read, follow, and navigate within the site due to the available whitespace included in the padding and space between elements.
