Site Plan

Name of Site

Salmon River Adventures

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to bring in customers and inform the public of the professional, high-end, safe and fun adventures provided by their experienced staff.

Target Audience

The target audience will be adventure enthusiasts that want the best River Rafting expierence available. This will include families and group adventure groups along with our single kayaking expierences.

PERSONA 1

NAME: Joe Wheatley

AGE: 38

PURPOSE OF VISIT: Joe is a father of 4. He enjoys outdoor adventures and is looking to get into kayaking and is looking for expierenced instructors to teach him. He also would like to take his family on a rafting adventure.

DEVICES USED: Joe will browse the site both with his iPhone 7.

PERSONA 2

NAME: Liz Stringham

AGE: 36

PURPOSE OF VISIT: Liz is a mother who wants her children to grow up outdoors. She plans two or three large adventures every summer and is looking to plan a white water rafting trip for her family. She wants a safe trip with expierenced staff as she has some young children.

DEVICES USED: Liz will be looking at the site on a computer at her house and wants ease of information prior to scheduling.

SCENARIO 1

Does Salmon River offer lessons with certified instructors?

The site will have a list of employees with credentials listed as well as expierences provided including lessons.

SCENARIO 2

Does Salmon River offer raft and kayak rentals?

We will have a list of products and adventures available.

SCENARIO 3

What is the current weather to plan for my adventure.

Current weather will be available on the home page so that you can check prior to leaving for your adventure and have up-to-date information.
